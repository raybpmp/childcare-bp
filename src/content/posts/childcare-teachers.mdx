# Hiring Childcare Teachers

In the childcare business, your product is people.
Hiring **Childcare Teachers** is not just an HR task; it is an inventory management task. Each teacher "unlocks" a specific number of revenue-generating slots (children).

*   **1 Infant Teacher** unlocks 4 Slots (Revenue: ~$6k).
*   **1 Preschool Teacher** unlocks 10 Slots (Revenue: ~$12k).

If you hire the teacher but do not fill the slots, you have "Inventory Spoilage." Use this guide to calculate the financial physics of your staff.

## The "Ratio Risk" Calculation
The most common point of failure for new Directors is **Ratio Inefficiency**.
*   **State Law:** 1 Teacher per 10 Preschoolers.
*   **Your Practice:** You have 11 kids. You are legally forced to hire a 2nd teacher.
*   **The Math:**
    *   Revenue: 11 kids * $1200 = $13,200.
    *   Labor: 2 Teachers * $3000 = $6,000.
    *   **The Margin:** Crashes from 60% to 50%.

You are paying a full salary for **1 child's worth of revenue**.
Efficient centers manage enrollment to strictly hit the "Max Ratio" (e.g., stopping enrollment at 10 until you have 18).

## Qualification Costs vs. Revenue
Higher qualifications demand higher salaries.
*   **Lead Teacher (CDA/degree):** $18-22/hr.
*   **Assistant (Entry):** $15-17/hr.

**The Strategy:**
You need **one** Lead Teacher to satisfy the "Educational" requirement of the room. The second staffer can be an Assistant.
Mixing these roles optimizes your "Blended Labor Rate" while maintaining compliance.

## The Director's Role
As a new owner, *you* are often the "Float."
If a teacher calls out, and you have to hire a sub agency ($30/hr), your profit for the day vanishes.
Smart owners certify themselves as Directors/Teachers to act as the ultimate "Free Substitute," protecting the bottom line.

## Conclusion: People, Not Just Payroll

Hiring isn't just about filling shifts; it's about building the human engine of your business. When you view teachers as "Revenue Unlockers" rather than just line-item expenses, your entire management philosophy changes.

Invest in finding, training, and retaining the right people. They are the ones who hold your brand, your reputation, and your business's future in their hands.
